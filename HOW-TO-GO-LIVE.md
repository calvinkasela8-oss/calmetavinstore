# Calmetavin Store - How to Go Live

Your store is ready! Here are simple ways to make it accessible to customers online.

---

## Option 1: Free Hosting with Netlify (Recommended - Easiest)

1. Go to https://www.netlify.com/
2. Sign up for a free account
3. Drag and drop your `Calmetavin-Store` folder onto the Netlify dashboard
4. Your site will be live at a URL like `calmetavin-store.netlify.app`
5. You can customize the URL in site settings

**Cost:** FREE

---

## Option 2: Free Hosting with GitHub Pages

1. Create a GitHub account at https://github.com/
2. Create a new repository named `calmetavin-store`
3. Upload all your files to the repository
4. Go to Settings > Pages > Select "main" branch
5. Your site will be live at `yourusername.github.io/calmetavin-store`

**Cost:** FREE

---

## Option 3: Free Hosting with Vercel

1. Go to https://vercel.com/
2. Sign up for free
3. Import your project folder
4. Your site will be live instantly

**Cost:** FREE

---

## Option 4: Local Testing (For Now)

Simply open `index.html` in your browser to test locally. Share the folder with customers who can open it on their devices.

---

## Before Going Live - Update These:

1. **WhatsApp Number:** Replace `265XXXXXXXX` with your actual phone number in:
   - `index.html` (contact section and WhatsApp button)
   - `products.html` (WhatsApp button)
   - `order.html` (WhatsApp button)
   - `styles.css` (footer contact info)

2. **Email Address:** Replace `info@calmetavinstores.com` with your actual email

3. **Physical Address:** Update `Lilongwe, Malawi` with your actual location

4. **Admin Password:** Change the default admin password:
   - Open `script.js`
   - Find `ADMIN_CREDENTIALS` near the top
   - Change `username` and `password` to your preferred values

---

## How Customers Will Access Your Store

Once hosted online, customers can:
- Visit your website URL on any device (phone, tablet, computer)
- Browse products on the Products page
- Add items to cart
- Place orders with their details
- Contact you via the WhatsApp button

## How You Manage Orders as Admin

- Go to `yourwebsite.com/admin.html`
- Login with your admin credentials
- View all customer orders
- Update order status (Pending, Processing, Completed)
- View customer information
- Export orders to CSV
- Check sales analytics
